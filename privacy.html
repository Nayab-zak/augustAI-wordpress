Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Overview

AugustAI ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through contact forms, emails, or during calls
  • Project Information: Details about your business processes, workflows, and automation needs
  • Payment Information: Billing details processed through secure third-party payment processors

Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, referral sources
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Cookies: Small files stored on your device to improve website functionality

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Deliver our AI automation and development services
  • Process payments and manage billing
  • Schedule meetings and consultations
  • Improve our website and services

Communication

  • Send project updates and service-related notifications
  • Provide technical support and maintenance updates
  • Send occasional marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Follow up on service inquiries and feedback requests

Data Security and Protection

Security Measures

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security updates
  • Access controls and authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Employee training on data protection practices

Client Project Data

  • Your business data remains in your systems unless transfer is required for project delivery
  • All access to client systems is logged and monitored
  • Project data is deleted from our systems upon project completion
  • Confidentiality agreements protect sensitive business information

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We Do Not Sell Your Data

We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited Sharing

We may share your information only in these specific circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Trusted partners who help deliver our services (e.g., email services, payment processors)

  • Consent: With your explicit permission for specific purposes

International Data Transfers

Cross-Border Operations
