Case Studies

UAE Logistics Company: Invoice Processing Automation

Problem

Processing 200+ supplier invoices monthly took 2 staff members 8 hours each. Manual data entry from PDFs to accounting system caused errors and delays.

Approach

Built an AI agent that reads email attachments, extracts invoice data, validates against purchase orders, and posts directly to their accounting system with approval workflows.

Result

Processing time reduced from 16 hours to 2 hours monthly. 90% of invoices now process automatically. ROI achieved in 6 weeks.

"The agent handles our entire invoice workflow. We just approve exceptions now. It's like having a super-efficient assistant that never makes mistakes."

UK SaaS Startup: Customer Success Dashboard

Problem

Customer success team couldn't identify at-risk accounts until churn happened. Data scattered across support tickets, usage analytics, and billing systems.

Approach

Created a unified dashboard pulling from Intercom, Mixpanel, and Stripe. Built predictive scoring model to flag at-risk customers 30 days before typical churn.

Result

Churn reduced by 40% in first quarter. Customer success team now proactively reaches out to at-risk accounts. Dashboard saves 10 hours weekly.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does "agentic AI" actually mean?
Agents that take action in your systems, not just provide advice. They log in, fetch data, make decisions based on rules you set, and update your systems automatically. Think robot employee, not smart chatbot.
How do you price projects?
Fixed scope, fixed fee. We agree on exactly what gets built and what acceptance criteria look like. No hourly billing, no scope creep. You know the total cost upfront.
What about data security and access?
Your data never leaves your systems unless required for processing. All agent actions are logged. Access is role-based with approval workflows. You can revoke access instantly at any time.
How do you work with UAE companies while based in Dubai?
I'm based in Dubai and meet clients locally. Invoicing is through our registered Pakistan entity to keep costs low. UAE business license available for enterprise clients who require it.
What if the acceptance criteria aren't met?
We fix it within the agreed sprint timeline at no extra cost. If we fundamentally can't deliver what was promised, you get a full refund. Binary success criteria prevent ambiguity.
What are typical project timelines?
Simple automations: 14 days. Dashboards: 10 days. Custom AI models: 3-6 weeks. Complex integrations: 4-8 weeks. We give exact timelines after the discovery call.
Do you only work with tech companies?
No. We've automated workflows for logistics, retail, consulting, and manufacturing companies. If you have repetitive tasks involving data, we can probably automate them.
What if our systems change after the project?
You own all the code and documentation. Basic changes can be handled by your team. For major updates, we offer maintenance packages or one-off modifications.
How do payments work?
50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery and acceptance. For larger projects, we can split into 3 payments based on milestones. All payments secured through proper invoicing.
Can you integrate with our existing software?
Most modern business software has APIs or export capabilities we can work with. CRM, accounting systems, email platforms, databases - if it has an API, we can connect to it.
